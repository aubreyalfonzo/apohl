CURRENT PROJECT(S)

PROJECT 01 / MS BLACK HISTORY POSTER

In honor of Black History Month, we will use our skills and tools as designers in order to highlight important black figures in Mississippi's history, from blues artists to activists.

Select a figure from the provided list (or propose a new addition) and conduct research. Your goal is to represent this figure in an appropriate, impactful, and compelling way, one that is formed by conceptual development and historical research.

The poster will be A1 format, plotter printed.
You are limited to black and white +/or 2-3 additional colors.
We are not looking to make traditional portraits, but to speak to these figures using informed visual language.

Refer to the Canvas assignment for the full brief and further requirements.

*MINI-PROJECT 01*

MINI PROJECT 01 / HORSE IN MOTION

This project will engage in some new software technology as well as examine an important moment in art and design history.

Eadweard Muybridge was a pioneer in motion capture and photography. His process was scientific in nature, a philosophy I find important to design.

We will be re-designing Muybridge’s most famous contribution, a series images known as “Horse in Motion.”

Each student will select a single frame, and redesign or stylize this image in any way that they wish, in order to create a new and exciting image on it’s own. (This image will be used in generating a new animation with Ebsynth).
